## PRD Summary

decoded is an image/item discovery platform where users find, tag, and share fashion and lifestyle items from images. The next version evolves this into a **Next Generation Magazine** — a curated, editorially-driven experience layered with AI recommendation. Users move from passive discovery to an immersive reading experience where content, context, and commerce are unified.

Core shift: from "browse images and find items" → "read curated stories, discover items in context, and act on them immediately."

## Target Strategy

| Dimension | Current | Next |
|-----------|---------|------|
| User mode | Active search / browse | Passive discovery + active engagement |
| Content format | Image grid + item spotting | Magazine-style editorial layouts |
| Curation model | User-uploaded + algorithmic feed | Editorial picks + AI-personalized feed |
| Commerce intent | Spot → Solution → Affiliate link | Visual Search → Intent detection → Commerce bridge |
| Creator role | Uploader + tagger | Editor + Stylist + Community curator |

**Target user:** Style-conscious 20–30s who follow trends, seek editorial context, and convert browsing to purchase. Values aesthetics, trust, and discovery serendipity.

## Tone & Manner

- **Editorial voice:** Confident, curated, not overwhelming. Speaks like a magazine editor, not an algorithm.
- **Visual language:** High-contrast, cinematic. Generous whitespace. Bold typography. Hero-led sections.
- **Interaction style:** Smooth, purposeful. Every transition communicates quality. No jank, no rush.
- **AI presence:** Invisible until needed. Surfaces recommendations and actions contextually, not intrusively.

## What This Changes

1. Home screen evolves to editorial hero sections (not just a grid)
2. Detail view adds "In This Issue" context layer (editorial narrative around an image)
3. Feed becomes a curated "Edition" (daily/weekly editorial batch, not chronological firehose)
4. Commerce entry points shift from spot-tap to visual search intent (see NEXT-04)
5. AI powers personalization and recommendation silently behind editorial shell (see NEXT-03)
